NASA's New Rocket Set To Launch June 2022

NASA's New Rocket

On the 17th of March, 2022, NASA's mighty SLS (Space Launch System) rolled out of the VAB (Vehicle Assembly Building) for the first time, to complete a series of tests, also called a WDR (Wet Dress Rehearsal). It completed those tests, but then went back to the VAB on the 17th of March, to repair various issues, improving the nitrogen supply from the GSE (Ground Supply Equipment), fixing a hydrogen leak and many others, however, the launch is still set for the launch window of June 6th-16th
